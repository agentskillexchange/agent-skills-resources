## Checklist

- [ ] The workflow is narrow and repeatable.

- [ ] Success criteria are written down.
- [ ] Human approval gates are defined.
- [ ] Rollback path is clear.
- [ ] Monitoring or review evidence is available.
- [ ] Security and permissions were reviewed.
- [ ] Users know when to stop and escalate.

## Decision Handoff

Use the readiness result to choose the next evidence artifact:

- If the pilot is not ready, record the blocker in `Next Action` and revisit this checklist after the owner, rollback path, or approval gates are clear.
- If the pilot is ready for a sandbox or limited-team run, open [`../templates/rollout-readiness.md`](../templates/rollout-readiness.md) before expanding scope so the next stage, owner, monitoring path, and rollback trigger are explicit.
- After the pilot finishes, use [`../templates/post-pilot-review.md`](../templates/post-pilot-review.md) to decide whether to stop, revise, continue, or expand based on evidence rather than momentum.
