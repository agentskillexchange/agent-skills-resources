# MLCommons AILuminate

MLCommons AILuminate is an AI safety benchmark effort. In the agent skills
ecosystem, it is relevant as safety-evaluation context for risk categories,
policy review, and rollout decisions.

## Where It Fits

- AI safety evaluation
- risk taxonomy orientation
- governance review
- safety benchmark context

## Skill-Adjacent Concepts

- risk categories
- safety evaluation
- policy review
- rollout evidence
- governance language

## Best Fit

- teams designing safety checks for agent workflows
- readers comparing security and governance resources
- policy discussions around model and workflow risk

## When Not To Use It

- as the only approval evidence for a production workflow
- for tool-call correctness
- without local security and permission review
