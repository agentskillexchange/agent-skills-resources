# NIST AI Risk Management Framework

NIST AI RMF provides risk management language and practices for AI systems.

## Where It Fits

It fits the governance layer. Use it when a team needs consistent language for
mapping, measuring, managing, and governing AI risk.

## Skill-Adjacent Concepts

- AI risk management
- governance
- measurement
- accountability
- monitoring
- policy and review cadence

## Best Fit

- team and enterprise governance
- defining review expectations
- connecting skill pilots to broader AI risk processes

## When Not To Use It

- as a quick technical test
- as a replacement for workflow-specific evidence
- as a security scanner
