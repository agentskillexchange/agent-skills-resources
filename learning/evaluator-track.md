# Evaluator Track

## Who It Is For

Maintainers, security reviewers, platform engineers, and team members deciding
whether a skill or resource is ready to use.

## Expected Time

60 minutes for the track, 15-30 minutes per skill review.

## Prerequisites

- Access to the skill or resource being reviewed.
- A target workflow or team use case.
- Enough context to judge data, repo, network, and production access.

## Practice Exercise

Review one skill or resource and record:

- source evidence
- setup clarity
- permissions needed
- verification evidence
- decision: reject, revisit, or pilot

## What Good Looks Like

- The decision is based on evidence, not brand familiarity or star counts.
- Risk is specific and reviewable.
- Missing evidence becomes a revisit item, not a quiet approval.

## Common Mistakes

- Treating install success as full verification.
- Missing command execution, network, or secret access.
- Ignoring stale links or unsupported framework claims.
