# Content Research Teams

## Who This Is For

Research, documentation, content, and technical marketing teams evaluating
skills for cited reports, long-form content workflows, and knowledge-base
publishing.

## When Agent Skills Are Useful

- Drafting cited research reports from collected sources.
- Running autonomous research with explicit source gathering.
- Producing long-form technical content with a repeatable workflow.
- Exporting knowledge-base content for publishing or downstream processing.

## When Not To Use Them Yet

- The workflow cannot distinguish cited claims from generated interpretation.
- Publishing is automatic with no human editorial review.
- Source ownership or licensing is unclear.

## First 30-Minute Evaluation

1. Review `draft-cited-research-reports-with-storm`.
2. Compare it with `run-autonomous-deep-research-workflows-with-gpt-researcher`.
3. Check whether citations are specific enough to audit.
4. Confirm the workflow marks unsupported claims as drafts.

## First Pilot Workflow

Choose one internal research brief. Gather sources, draft with STORM or GPT
Researcher, then use
`build-versioned-technical-docs-sites-with-search-and-nav-using-material-for-mkdocs`
or `export-obsidian-vaults-into-clean-markdown-trees-for-publishing-or-downstream-processing`
for publishing prep.

## Trust And Safety Checklist

- Every factual claim has a source or is labeled as interpretation.
- Source labels distinguish official, lab, community, and ASE references.
- Human editors review before external publication.
- The workflow avoids stale popularity claims and unsupported comparisons.

## Rollout Path

- Sandbox: draft from public sources only.
- Limited team: use for internal briefs with editorial review.
- Production: require citation audit, source labeling, and publication approval.

## Metrics To Track

- Citation accuracy.
- Unsupported claims caught in review.
- Time from brief to reviewed draft.
- Editorial corrections by type.

## Common Failure Modes

- Overstating source claims.
- Treating community material as official.
- Publishing generated summaries without citation review.
